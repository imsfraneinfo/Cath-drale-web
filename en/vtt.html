VTT route

River Road through Iminouarg, Tamga and Tilouguite

The route follows the road beside the river through Douar Iminouarg, Douar Tamga and Tilouguite. During the ride, you discover Imsfrane by bicycle, enjoy the mountain landscapes and pass through peaceful local villages.

  • Road beside the river
  • Discovery of Douar Iminouarg
  • Passage through Douar Tamga
  • Discovery of Tilouguite
  • Photo and rest stops
  • Accompanied by our guide
Important: The guide chooses the appropriate distance according to the selected duration, the group level and route conditions.
