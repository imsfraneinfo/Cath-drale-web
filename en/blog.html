Jebel Imsfrane – The Cathedral of the Atlas

Jebel Imsfrane is one of the most remarkable natural landmarks in the region. Rising to 1,868 metres, its impressive rock formation resembles a giant cathedral.

The mountain is surrounded by forests, river valleys and traditional villages, making it an exceptional destination for hiking, photography and nature lovers.
