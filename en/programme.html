Complete Atlas experience

Three days between mountains, river and stars

Discover Imsfrane Cathédrale, climb to the 1,868-metre summit, explore Wakoudene waterfalls and spend two nights in our wild camping area. The program combines nature, local food, adventure and Amazigh hospitality.

3 days 2 wild camping nights
1,868 m Imsfrane summit
8–30 Recommended group
Moderate Adapted to the group
Our wild camping

Sleep under the Atlas stars

The two nights are spent in our own wild camping area near Imsfrane. We prepare the tents, mattresses and blankets before the group arrives.

  • Equipped tents
  • Mattresses and blankets
  • Campfire atmosphere
  • Traditional meals
  • Clear mountain sky
  • Night electricity available

This is an outdoor wild-camping experience. Services remain simple and close to nature.

Detailed itinerary

Your three-day adventure

01

Arrival and wild camping

Welcome to Imsfrane

Day 1
  • Welcome and meeting with the local team near Tilouguite or Imsfrane.
  • Transfer to our wild camping area and installation in the tents.
  • Welcome tea and introduction to the three-day program.
  • Short discovery walk around the camp, river and surrounding landscape.
  • Traditional dinner prepared at the camp.
  • First night in our equipped wild-camping tents.

Evening around the fire with tea, music and a peaceful atmosphere under the stars.

02

Imsfrane summit and Wakoudene waterfalls

Main adventure day

Day 2
  • Traditional breakfast at the camp.
  • 4x4 transfer through the mountain tracks toward the starting point.
  • Guided ascent to Jebel Imsfrane, reaching an altitude of 1,868 metres.
  • Panoramic views and photo break at the summit.
  • Picnic or local lunch in nature.
  • Visit to Wakoudene waterfalls and natural pools, depending on the season.
  • Return to the wild camp, rest and dinner.

Traditional evening with guitar, Ahidouss music and a campfire.

03

Activities and departure

Final day

Day 3
  • Wake up in the mountains and enjoy the morning landscape.
  • Breakfast at the wild camp.
  • Optional rafting descent on Oued Ahansal.
  • Optional VTT ride through local trails.
  • Optional quad excursion in the mountain area.
  • Lunch before departure.
  • End of the program and departure according to the group’s transport plan.
