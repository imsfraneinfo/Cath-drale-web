Imsfrane Cathédrale · Tilouguite

Wild Camping Under the Stars

An authentic night in the Atlas Mountains, surrounded by the river, mountains and stars.

Leave everything behind and dive into the wild. Here, there are no walls and no city noise—only you, the river, the mountain and a sky full of stars.

We provide the traditional tent (mfercha), mattresses and blankets. You only need to bring your personal belongings and your adventurous spirit.

It is an unforgettable experience at the foot of Imsfrane Cathédrale, far from the stress of the city, where you can reconnect with nature and the essentials.
