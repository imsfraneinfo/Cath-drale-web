Route 3 · 50 km · 2 days

Two-Day Rafting Expedition

A complete expedition combining rafting, mobile bivouac, traditional meals, Amazigh heritage and the option to continue to Bin El Ouidane.

1 Day One

  • Meeting at your location, your accommodation or our camping.
  • Distribution of all safety equipment.
  • Transfer by 4x4 to Amougr, the expedition starting point.
  • Briefing, instructions and practical safety test.
  • Start of the rafting descent.
  • Photos, videos and time to enjoy the river and nature.
  • Our mobile bivouac travels by 4x4 toward Ighrman to prepare lunch.
  • Lunch at Ighrman followed by an afternoon rest.
  • Light hike in Douar Aït Hssine to discover old Amazigh architecture built several centuries ago.
  • Visit to a traditional, hand-operated olive oil press.
  • Dinner, campfire evening and night in the bivouac.

2 Day Two

  • Breakfast at the bivouac.
  • Preparation and putting on the rafting equipment.
  • Continuation of the rafting journey toward Sidi Youssef.
  • Passage through beautiful gorges with cliffs reaching several tens of metres.
  • Arrival at Sidi Youssef Bridge, with the choice to finish here or continue to Bin El Ouidane.

Option 1 — Sidi Youssef Bridge

End of the rafting descent at Sidi Youssef Bridge, followed by return in 4x4 to your accommodation.

2 500 DH / Person

Option 2 — Bin El Ouidane Dam

Continue to the end of Oued Ahansal. A large boat meets the group on the lake. Lunch is served on board, followed by return from Bin El Ouidane in 4x4.

3 000 DH / Person
